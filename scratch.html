Food ideas by macro

This numbered list includes bulleted lists inside it.

  1. Protein options
    • Chicken, turkey, lean beef, eggs, Greek yogurt, protein shakes
    • Protein helps with muscle repair and feeling full.
  2. Carb options
    • Rice, potatoes, oats, fruit, sourdough, tortillas, whole-grain pasta
    • Carbs help fuel workouts, walking, and daily energy.
  3. Healthy fat options
    • Avocado, olive oil, salmon, nuts, nut butter, eggs, chia seeds
    • Fats support hormones, flavor, and balanced meals.

Meal and workout rhythm

I like the idea of building routines that feel strong but still realistic. My baseline goals are at least 10,000 steps a day and strength training four times a week.

Breakfast

Greek yogurt, berries, granola, and eggs.

Lunch

Chicken bowl with rice, veggies, avocado, and salsa.

Training

Upper, lower, rest or steps, then repeat.
